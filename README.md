# Teh Ais Station Sales Dashboard

## Objective
Analyze transaction data from a milk tea business to understand sales trends, customer behavior and product performance.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel / CSV dataset

## Dataset
387 real transaction records from a milk tea stall.

## Key Insights
- Peak sales occur between 4 PM – 5 PM
- Most customers purchase the 22 oz cup
- Only 27% of customers are recurring
- Weekend sales are unpredictable and influenced by nearby events

## Business Recommendations
- Prepare more ingredients before peak hours
- Introduce loyalty program to increase recurring customers
- Encourage add-ons to increase revenue
